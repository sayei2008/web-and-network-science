Springboard

List of Courses

LYIT is delighted to announce 12 new Springboard+ courses for the academic year 2018/2019 in the areas of culinary, computing, technology and finance.

To view a full list of all Springboard courses click here.

Application Process

The Springboard initiative in Higher Education offers free courses at Certificate, Degree and Masters level leading to qualification in areas where there are employment opportunities in the economy.

The courses are aimed at unemployed persons who, subject to meeting certain criteria, will retain any payments they are in receipt of from the Department of Social Protections.

Once you click on the relevant Springboard course, you can apply through the relevant course page. 

Springboard Explained

Check out LYIT's Springboard video. Our staff and students provide insight into what Springboard is and outline the benefits from enrolling in a Springboard programme at LYIT.
