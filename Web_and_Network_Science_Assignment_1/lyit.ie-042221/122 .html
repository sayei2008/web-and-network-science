Academic Life

Our aim is for you to achieve your potential academically and enjoy a rich, rewarding learning experience with us.  During your time at LYIT you will have the chance to develop academically, professionally and personally. 

We understand that adjusting to third level can be different for every student.  There are numerous activities and resources in academic development to help you.  These include guidance on study techniques, writing and IT, available at any stage of your learning journey.

Remember everyone at LYIT wants you to succeed; lecturers, support staff and students are all here to help.  So, there’s always someone to talk to.  The  college experience is all about getting involved and getting active, trying something new, having fun and making friends along the way.  Choose to get involved in campus life, access support if you need it, so that you experience everything that LYIT has to offer. 

For more information on the range of activities and services that can assist you, browse the links within this section.
