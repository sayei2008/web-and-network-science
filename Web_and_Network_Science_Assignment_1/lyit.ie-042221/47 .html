Open Days / Campus Tours

Our Open Day, Open Evenings and Information Day provide prospective students with an ideal opportunity to find out more about our Degree programmes.

Staff from our various departments will be available at each of our Open Day and Open Evening events. It is also a chance to speak to students and explore the outstanding facilities available at LYIT.

Local schools usually bus their students to the event, but it is not just an event for school-leavers or indeed for locals, everyone, including mature and second chance learners are welcome to attend. 

We are also very happy to accommodate visitors from outside Donegal, whether individually or in groups. Parents of prospective students are also welcome to attend.
