Sports & Recreation

It’s not all about study.  Striving for success on the pitch or volunteering for something you believe in, all contribute to an enriching and rounded educational experience. 

 

It’s good to take time out to indulge your interests, get some head space and take advantage of some of the excellent facilities on campus. 

There are endless possibilities to get involved in student life at LYIT.  Clubs and societies are the perfect way to make friends and develop skills or interests, some of which could be useful for a career.  LYIT has everything from sports to debating, volunteering and charities.  For elite athletes, LYIT offers Sports Awards and Scholarships.

Jump in.  Get involved.  Activities outside formal study can help to develop your personal and professional skills.  You’ll be sure to find some inspiration within this section.  Enjoy.
