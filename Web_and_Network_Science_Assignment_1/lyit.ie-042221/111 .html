Central Services

If you need any help with practical, non-academic related issues at LYIT, then contact our administration staff. We have a full range of administrative services, for example:

Information Resource Centre

The Library is at the academic heart of the Institute. It is located on the ground floor of the LYIT to the left of the main entrance door. All registered students have full access to the library and services provided until 1 September of the following year. The Library endeavours to support the educational and research needs of staff and students. 

Estates 

The Estates department′s role is to provide buildings, grounds and facilities of the highest standard to the staff, students and the research community of the Institute, in a professional and safe manner.

Contact: +353 (0)74 918 6112 and maintenancerequests@lyit.ie. 

Finance

The Finance section provides a range of services including:

  • Payroll processing 
  • Managing the purchasing function of the Institute
  • Invoicing customers of the Institute
  • Monitoring compliance with relevant legislation
  • Safeguarding the fixed assets of the Institute
  • Providing financial reports to internal and external stakeholders
  • Involvement in the payment of student grants.

Prompt Payment Reports, pursuant to Government Decision No S29296 of 2 and 8 March 2011 and 28 March 2017, can be found here.

The Finance offices are located in Room 1159 of the main building (Letterkenny campus), and queries can be addressed to accounts@lyit.ie. 

Computer Services 

The Computer Services department′s role within this is to provide technical services of the highest quality to the staff, students and the research community of the Institute, in a professional, legal and ethical manner.
