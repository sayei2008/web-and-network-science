Our Students & Campuses

Explore campus life at LYIT and our state-of-the-art facilities.

LYIT is an organisation that likes to move with the times. We realise our role in providing opportunities for all of those who study with us; we have to be proactive in developing a suite of courses that give the best opportunity to progress in life. It is also important that our campuses and facilities reflect the growing needs of a third level students

There is more to college than attending lectures and at LYIT we are proud of the community spirit that has developed on campus, whether in Letterkenny or Killybegs, that makes us different from other third level institutions. Take time to read some stories from past graduates and find out information about our two campuses.
