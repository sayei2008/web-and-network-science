Graduations

Conferring of Awards

Welcome to the LYIT Conferring of Awards section.  This year's graduation ceremonies take place on Friday, 26 October 2018 (Letterkenny campus) and Thursday, 1 November 2018 (Killybegs campus).  Scroll down to find out more.

Letterkenny Campus 

The graduation ceremonies for the Letterkenny campus take place on Friday, 26 October 2018, in An Dánlann, as follows -

Time School/Department graduation
10.00 am Department of Design & Creative Media
Department of Law & Humanities
Department of Business Studies
2.00 pm Department of Nursing & Health Studies
Department of Civil Engineering & Construction
Department of Electronic & Mechanical Engineering
Lifelong Learning
5.00 pm Department of Science
Department of Computing

You will receive an invitation to the ceremony for you and two guests.  

Academic Dress

Each graduand will be required to wear academic dress.  This can be hired from our supplier at a cost of €25 per gown and hood, and there are two methods of booking -

  • Book and pay in advance at www.armstrongandoxford.com (online booking available c.16 October).
  • Pay, by credit/debit card, for your gown on the day.  The gowning rooms are located in the Main Building and will be open for business at the following times -
Session Gowning date/time
Session 1 Thursday, 25 October: 2 - 6 pm 
Friday, 26 October: 8 am
Session 2 Friday, 26 October: 12 pm (no earlier)
Session 3 Friday, 26 October: 3 pm (no earlier)
Graduate Destination Survey

The process for conducting our annual Graduate Destination Survey has changed.  All Higher Education Institutions throughout Ireland have now adopted a uniform format.  From April 2019, you will be sent an email inviting you to complete the short survey online.  Your responses will be very important as they will help to determine planning for future course provision and influence regional employment policy.  The graduate survey will also enable you to provide valuable feedback on your experience as a student at LYIT.  All data is anonymised, so no personal or identifying information will be shared.  Personal information about named individuals will also not be shared.  If you are in agreement for the college to contact you regarding the graduate survey, please click on this link. 

Seating Plan for Conferring

A Conferring Seating Plan will be available here prior to the conferring.  We ask all graduands to check their seat allocation before the ceremony.  Please ensure that you are seated in the hall 20 minutes before the commencement of your session for a brief rehearsal. 

Photography

A professional photography service will be available in the Institute; however, graduands are not obliged to avail of the facility.  Photography studios will be open before and after each ceremony, and a booking may be made in advance at www.lafayette.ie (from 16 October onwards).  On the day bookings can be paid by credit/debit card.

Non-attendance

If you are unable to attend the Conferring of Awards, you may collect your parchment from the Examinations Office or Registry Office at LYIT from 12 November 2018.  Alternatively, your parchment will be posted to you.

Killybegs Campus 

The graduation ceremony for the Killybegs campus takes place on Thursday, 1 November 2018, at 11 am in The Blue Haven, Largymore, Kilcar.  You will receive an invitation to the ceremony for you and two guests.  

Academic Dress

Each graduand will be required to wear academic dress.  This can be hired from our supplier at a cost of €25 per gown and hood, and there are two methods of booking -

  • Book and pay in advance at www.armstrongandoxford.com (online booking available up to 3 days before graduation day).
  • Pay, by cash only, for your gown on the day.  Gowns will be available in The Blue Haven from 9 am.
Graduate Destination Survey

The process for conducting our annual Graduate Destination Survey has changed.  All Higher Education Institutions throughout Ireland have now adopted a uniform format.  From April 2019, you will be sent an email inviting you to complete the short survey online.  Your responses will be very important as they will help to determine planning for future course provision and influence regional employment policy.  The graduate survey will also enable you to provide valuable feedback on your experience as a student at LYIT.  All data is anonymised, so no personal or identifying information will be shared.  Personal information about named individuals will also not be shared.  If you are in agreement for the college to contact you regarding the graduate survey, please click on this link. 

Photography

A professional photography service will be available in the hotel; however, graduands are not obliged to avail of the facility.  Photography studios will be open before and after the ceremony, and a booking may be made in advance at www.lafayette.ie (online booking available up to 1 day prior to your ceremony).  Payment on the day is by cash only.

Non-attendance

If you are unable to attend the Conferring of Awards, you may collect your parchment from the School Office from 6 November 2018.  Alternatively, your parchment will be posted to you.
