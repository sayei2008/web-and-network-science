Work Placement & Projects

We take pride in the fact that work placement is a large feature of a large number of our programmes here at LYIT.

We have a cross discipline of sectors from business to computing, to health to sport, to culinary and hospitality. So generally there is work placement opportunities to suit all types of businesses. 

If you would like to find out how you might be able to avail of a work placement or project opportunity for your business, contact the relevant person listed in the various Schools. 

School of Business

The School of Business has various opportunities within their Departments of Business, Design & Creative Media and Law & Humanities. Click here to view opportunities for work placements in each department.

School of Engineering

The School of Engineering has various opportunities within the Departments of Civil Engineering & Construction. Click here to view opportunities for work placements within this department.

School of Tourism 

The School of Tourism has various opportunities within their Department of Hospitality, Tourism & Culinary Arts. Click here to view opportunities for work placements within this school.

School of Science 

The School of Science has various opportunities within their Departments of Computing, Science and Nursing and Health Studies. Click here to view opportunities for work placements within each department
