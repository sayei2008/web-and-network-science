Fees & Scholarships

Non-EU Students

Non-EU students are required to pay the current Non-EU fee rate for Undergraduate and Postgraduate Courses. This is €10,000 per year.  

If you are unsure of your fee status click here. 

LYIT may offer partial Scholarships to qualifying students in accordance with our strategic priorities and the academic and other credentials of the student. This is a limited programme and does not apply to all academic areas.  LYIT do not offer full scholarships.

Pay to Study

LYIT recommends that international students use Pay to Study for payment.  Pay to Study is an easy to use online payment system.  It offers a fast and secure way to make international payments at no cost to the payer or the payee.  Please click here to make payments via Pay to Study.
