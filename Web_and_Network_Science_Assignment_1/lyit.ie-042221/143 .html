Health Services

Welcome to the LYIT Health Service webpage.  We offer a full general practice service free to all registered students with an emphasis on health promotion, with a daily GP surgery and a full-time nurse.

There are two doctors who cover the weekly surgery - Dr Mary McFadden and Dr David Khavia, and one full-time registered nurse - Hannah Glackin.

All information provided by the doctor or nurse is confidential and is not disclosed to any other person, without the permission of the student concerned.  All records are kept on a separate network to the main system and are not shared. Preventative medicine and screening are also available.

The opening hours for the Health Services are listed in the Frequently Asked Questions link below.  You can make the most of the service by arriving on time for your appointment or contacting the Student Service Reception to cancel if you are unable to attend.  If you have any long-term illness which requires regular monitoring or medication, please register with the medical service as soon as possible.

The college nurse and doctor is located in the College Health Centre on the first floor of An Dánlann in the Student Services Suite. An appointment systems is in operation but emergencies will always be catered for. To make an appointment, contact the receptionist at the Student Services. You may also contact our service via email but please remember that email is the property of the Institute and is therefore not confidential.

Emergency Out-of-Hours Cover

Emergency out-of-hours cover is provided by:

NowDOC - Telephone 1850 400 911 
Opening hours are midday on Saturday, all day Sunday and Bank Holidays.  
Weekends 6 pm to 8 am.  Students who do not hold a medical card will be charged for the NowDOC service.

Dr McFadden's Surgery - Telephone 074 912 9393 
Emergency cover, 8 - 9 am and 5 - 6 pm, Monday - Friday.
