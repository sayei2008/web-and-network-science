Degree/Diploma/Certificate/ACCS

Application Process

To apply for any part-time undergraduate programme (greater than 60 ECTS) please download the following application form: Direct Entry Application Form

To apply for any part-time programme (up to 60 ECTS) please download the following application form: Lifelong Learning Application Form. 

Please return the completed application form to:

Registry Office
Letterkenny Institute of Technology
Port Road
Letterkenny
Co Donegal
F92 FC93

or submit by e-mail to admissions@lyit.ie.

Entry Requirements

Details of entry requirements are provided for each course under the relevant course page. In addition relevant experience or qualifications may also be necessary for some courses, all of which will be outlined under the relevant course page. 

If you have any specific queries about any Degree, Diploma, Certificate programmes or ACCS please contact the relevant head of department. Contact details are outlined in the relevant programme page.
