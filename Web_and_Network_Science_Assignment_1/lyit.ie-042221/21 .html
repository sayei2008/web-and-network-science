How to Apply for Full-time Undergraduate Courses

Depending on what type of applicant you are, the application process will differ. We have included different links in the navigation pane. Click on the link which bests describes your application status. 

This section will assist on how to apply depending on your situation. Regardless, of whether you are a leaving certificate applicant or a mature applicant, the admissions staff are there to help with a range of practical advice.

If you cannot find the information on our website, the admission staff can be contacted by phone on +353 (0)74 918 6000, by e-mail admissions@lyit.ie, or by coming to the main reception, in person.
