Alumni

The friends you make in college are often friends for life. Equally, the bond you have for your college years is one that we hope you have forever.

It’s why as a graduate of LYIT you’re never forgotten, and we hope you won’t forget us by contacting with our alumni association.

Mutually Beneficial Relationship 

There are a number of ways we like to try and connect with our alumni, which is mutually beneficial, including the following: 

  • Providing exposure for firms from an employment perspective for your firm e.g. at our Careers Graduate & Recruitment Fair
  • Providing research and support where necessary for business start-ups
  • Bringing alumni back as a guest speaker to share their experience
  • Providing work placement opportunities to our students
  • Providing employment opportunities to our students 
  • Assist the college with testimonials and at times garner exposure for the college throught alumni profiles

If you would like to discuss any of the above or find out more, email alumni@lyit.ie for further information. We always like to hear from our alumni.
