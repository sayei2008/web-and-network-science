Taught Masters/Postgraduate Diplomas/Higher Diplomas

List of Courses

To view a full list of all Taught Masters/Postgraduate & Higher Diplomas click here.

Application Process

To apply for any Taught Masters or Postgraduate/Higher Diploma please download the following application form: Postgraduate Application Form

Please return the completed application form to:

Registry Office
Letterkenny Institute of Technology
Port Road
Letterkenny
Co Donegal
F92 FC93

or submit by e-mail to admissions@lyit.ie.

Get in Touch

If you have any specific queries about Taught Masters, Postgraduate Diplomas or Higher Diplomas please contact the relevant head of department. Contact details are outlined in the relevant programme page.

Postgraduate Scholarships 

Find out more about 'Pushing Boundaries', a scholarship initiative specifically aimed towards applicants interested in applying for Full-time Postgraduate Masters for the academic year 2018/2019. For further information please click here.
