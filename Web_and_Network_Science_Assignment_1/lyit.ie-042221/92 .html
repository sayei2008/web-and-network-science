Sports Centre & Room Hire

Sports Centre Hire

LYIT hall measures 32 x 28 mts and has retractable seating for 344. Additional seating may be added. 
It is available for sports and event hire at certain times of the year, when available, outside of academic and Institute needs.

To find out more about hiring the sports centre, contact Helen at helen.kennedy@lyit.ie or +353 (0)74 918 6868.

Sports Centre Hire Fees

For information and cost regarding facility hire, please click here.

Room Hire

LYIT has a variety of classrooms, lecture theatres and IT Labs which are available for booking outside of core teaching time (9.30 am - 5.30 pm, Monday - Friday).  To find out more about hiring rooms at LYIT, contact Lorraine at lorraine.kessack@lyit.ie or +353 (0)74 918 6112.
