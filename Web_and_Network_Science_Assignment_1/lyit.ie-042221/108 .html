Executive Board

The Executive Board is the senior management team within the Institute.  The Board works on a collegiate basis, striving to achieve consensus decision making on the major challenges facing the Institute on a strategic basis.  Each member of the Board has responsibility for a designated functional area allowing two-way communication throughout the Institute on all relevant matters.

Membership of the Executive Board:

Name Title
Paul Hannigan President
Billy Bennett VP for Academic Affairs and Registrar
John Andy Bonar VP for Research, Equality and External Affairs
Henry McGarvey VP for Finance and Corporate Services
Dr Seán Duffy Head of School of Tourism
Denis McFadden Head of School of Engineering
Michael Margey Head of School of Business
Dr Gertie Taggart Head of School of Science
