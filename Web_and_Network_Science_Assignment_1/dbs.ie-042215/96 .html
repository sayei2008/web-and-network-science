Welcome to the Registrar’s Office

Thank you for choosing to study with Dublin Business School. We hope you will have a happy and rewarding experience with us. Here in Dublin Business School, we endeavour to ensure all interactions between students and the Registrar’s Office as smooth and hassle free as possible.
On this page you will find all relevant information for your course along with online forms to ensure your requests are dealt with in a timely manner.

Online Student Forms

Students can now fill out any of the below forms and submit them online.

  • Letter Request Form
  • Request Duplicate Transcripts
  • Course Change Request Form
  • Receipt for Tax Relief Form
  • Change of Details Form
  • Request Student Card
  • Receipt for Fees Paid
  • Request Parchment

For the following forms, please complete and return by email. 

  • Personal Mitigating Circumstances (PMC) Form 2016-17
  • Assignment Extension Request Form
  • Company Sponsorship Form
