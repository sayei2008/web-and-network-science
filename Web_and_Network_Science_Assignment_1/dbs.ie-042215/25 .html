International

DBS will be accepting students to start studying in January 2019 for a number of Full-Time Postgraduate Programmes including the Master of Business Administration and corresponding General, Marketing, HRM, Cloud Computing and Finance programmes.

International Students can also apply for the January 2019 intake of the BA (Hons) in Business and BA (Hons) Marketing Full-Time Undergraduate Programmes. See below for course information and to apply. 
 
Study Business, Accountancy, Arts or Law in Dublin Business School, Ireland. Dublin Business School is Ireland’s largest independent third-level college. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and professional programmes in Business, Arts, Law and Professional Accountancy (ACCA) to students from Ireland, Europe and countries outside the EU.

Dublin Business School extends a warm Irish welcome to all international partners visiting this site. DBS has over the last 36 years, established itself as a centre for international education excellence. The College has developed strategic links with a significant number of third level institutions in Europe, the US and Asia and has students currently from over 70 countries. Over the years the School has established partnership agreements with nearly 100 universities worldwide offering students the opportunity to attend for semester, dual degree and Masters Programmes and receive ECTS credits. Located in the heart of Dublin’s City centre, DBS aims to ensure that your students will have a rewarding and challenging experience as well as experiencing the culture and sights of a vibrant capital city. As per Irish Student Visa Regulations non-EU student must be enrolled in full-time, day-time academic courses leading to a major award as outlined on the National Framework of Qualifications.
