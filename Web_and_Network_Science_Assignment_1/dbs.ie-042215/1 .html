Evening Degree

Student Life

DBS students have a range of sports clubs and societies available to join, as well as a thriving student community in the college. 

Why Choose Dublin

Dublin's strength and success comes from its diversity and unique mix of cultures. The city is constantly changing - retaining a sense of tradition while also embracing the new. 

DBS International Programmes

The International Department at Dublin Business School offers a wide range of Undergraduate and Postgraduate Programmes in the areas of Business, Arts, Law, Psychology, Computing and more. Programmes are awarded by Quality & Qualifications Ireland (QQI).

Employability

DBS's programmes have been designed and developed in collaboration with industry and the professional bodies so that on graduation in addition to your academic qualifications, you will have the skills and knowledge for a successful career.

Careers

DBS helps you to develop your abilities and broaden your experience so you leave your course ready for your chosen career.  

Learning Environment

In addition to over 50,000 print books, DBS Library provides access to an extensive collection of online books, journals and databases. To support the student in their learning, lecture notes and support materials are also available via the virtual learning environment Moodle.

Professional Accountancy

As part of Kaplan, Dublin Business School (DBS) has an unrivalled reputation as one of Ireland’s leading colleges to study professional accountancy. DBS offers comprehensive tuition options to students of ACCA and CIMA.

Why choose DBS?


 LADDER OF OPPORTUNITY

 CITY CENTRE LOCATION

STUDENT SUPPORT
