We are holding an ACCA Information Evening on February 5th at 6.30pm in our Castle House building. 

This ACCA information evening is a great opportunity to learn more about our innovative delivery options, meet with DBS ACCA lecturers and find out more about the student focused DBS ACCA offering. To register for this event please click here. 
Delivering Success

Dublin Business School has a strong track record of success in the provision of Professional Accountancy education. Powered by Kaplan Financial, today we offer expertise in flexible delivery, giving you the freedom to manage your studies where, when and how it suits you, under the guidance of tutors you can trust to help you to pass first time.

As a Platinum partner of ACCA and the only official CIMA publisher worldwide, you can be confident that you will receive the highest standard of training no matter which qualification you choose to pursue.

ACCA

  • All courses include ACCA approved learning materials.

  • Online pass rates of 83%, 30% higher than the global average. 

  • Expertise in online learning delivery - Kaplan combines 70 years of educational and learning excellence with leading online learning technologies.

CIMA

  • The only official CIMA publisher worldwide, all our materials are reviewed and approved by the CIMA faculty.

  • Up to 94% CIMA pass rates*.

*Pass rate achieved in E2 Objective Tests taken between 1st March 2016 – 30th September 2016 from a total of 178 students.

Why Study With DBS


Flexibility

Choose from 4 flexible ways to study.

Plan ahead with multiple start dates available across all 4 sittings

Support

Access to MyKaplan - your personalised online study space

Get instant support via Livechat, or a response within 4 hours via email or request a callback

Confidence

Pass rates up to 100%*

*Based on ACCA F5-F9, P1-P3, P6 and P7 papers for part time classroom students across 14 Kaplan centres at the June 2017 sitting.

Ways to Study

Classroom

Face-to-face learning at Dublin Business School, taught by expert tutors on a weekday, weekend or evening. The most structured way to learn.

Currently only available for ACCA
Live Online

The nearest thing to sitting in a classroom. Join timetabled live lectures online and post questions for live tutor feedback. Gives you structure with the option to watch recorded lectures in your own time.
OnDemand

Study when it suits you with an efficient tutor-led online course using resources designed specifically to keep you motivated and engaged. Combines structure and flexibility.

 

Distance Learning

Self study with printed and online materials for you to work through at your own pace, with tutor support available when you need it. The most flexible way to study.
