Business and Management Courses

Studying Business and Management courses at Dublin Business School (DBS) will give you a wide range of valuable learnings and skills for one of today’s most demanding yet rewarding career paths and working environments.

The full-time and part-time undergraduate degree, postgraduate  masters and evening diploma courses we offer in Business and Management allow you to acquire both general and specialised knowledge and skills in a number of key business and management areas, including:

  • Business Administration

  • Marketing

  • Business Management

  • Human Resources

  • Information Systems.

Our degrees are awarded by the Quality Qualifications Ireland - QQI.

The key to your bright business career

Your qualification from DBS will open up the world of business and its wide range of fulfilling and rewarding career opportunities. The acquired depth of knowledge and understanding may be applied across multiple disciplines and can lead you along a wide variety of career paths.

Outstanding teaching staff and learning environment

At DBS all your potential for business and management will be fully realised in a learning environment that is tailor-made to deliver the highest standards of graduate and postgraduate education. This is achieved through a comprehensive program of lectures, seminars, tutorials, case studies, group and individual projects, examinations, reports and continuous assessment.
