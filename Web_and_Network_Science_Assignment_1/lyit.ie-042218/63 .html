Careers Service

Our Careers Service helps you make a smooth transition from college life to graduate employment by offering impartial confidential advice on successful career planning whilst you’re still in college and on your options for when you leave LYIT.

We offer the following services: individual appointments/ group talks on employment, postgraduate studies, taking a year out and clarifying your career options if you have no definite idea of what your next step should be.

You can avail of the opportunity to meet recruiters and postgraduate course tutors at our annual Careers and Postgraduate Studies Fair.


Each year we’ve loads of initiatives including workshops, seminars and mock interviews, talks from key employers and recent graduates that are as inspiring as they are informative. Practical advice is offered on everything from how to craft your CV and writing great cover letters to setting up your own business.

If you’re keen to progress your studies to the next level, we’ve a wealth of information on further options and the funding available in both Ireland, UK, Europe and further afield. We’re located upstairs in An Dánlann in the Student Services Suite. For more information call the Careers Office on +353 (0)74 918 6812 or careers@lyit.ie.
