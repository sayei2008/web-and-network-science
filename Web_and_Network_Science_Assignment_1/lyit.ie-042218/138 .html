Sports Centre

Sports and recreational facilities are located on campus in the multi-purpose centre (An Dánlann).  Students, staff and members of the general public are most welcome to avail of the facilities.

 The opening times* during Term Time are:

Monday 8 am - 9.30 pm
Tuesday 8 am - 9.30 pm
Wednesday 8 am - 9.30 pm
Thursday 8 am - 9.30 pm
Friday 8 am - 7.45 pm
Saturday** 11 am - 3 pm (term time only)

* Please note that closing times in June, July and August are earlier (6 pm).
** National League Basketball Matches monthly on Sat evenings throughout the year.

The hall may be unavailable due to Institute needs at certain periods throughtout the academic year, eg, graduation, careers fair, etc.

Exercise Classes

Entry to the fitness suite and use of the hall is FREE for full-time students, and there is a small charge for exercise classes.  To view a list of exercise classes available please click here.
