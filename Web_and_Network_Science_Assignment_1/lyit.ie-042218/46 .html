Fees, Grants & Scholarships

The Admissions Office is responsible for the administration of Fees and Grants for all students at LYIT.  It is important to know how much your course might cost, and if financial supports are available to you during your time in college.  

By clicking on the links below you will find the information you need about course fees and the financial supports available.

The Admissions Office is located on the ground floor adjacent to the Main Reception and is open from 9.00 – 1.00 and 2.00 – 5.00, Monday to Friday.  For fee queries call to the Admissions Office or contact us at +353 (0)74 918 6125 / 6129.
