Careers & Opportunities

Careers Information Service

Welcome to the Careers Services centre.  We're here to help students interested in planning and developing their career.  We offer the opportunity to make the most of your transition from college to graduate employment by offering impartial, confidential advice on your options when you leave LYIT. 

The Careers Services are located upstairs in An Dánlann in the Student Services suite.  The Careers Officer is available Monday to Friday during office hours. There is a drop-in service each morning from 10 am until 12 noon for quick queries and by appointment at other times.  You can make an appointment by telephoning the Careers Officer at 074 918 6812 leaving a short message and a contact telephone number, or by contacting the Receptionist at the Student Services Suite on 074 918 6855.

You will be guaranteed a friendly and efficient service.  All telephone enquiries will be responded to within 2 working days and all email enquiries will be dealt with within 3 working days. 

Fáiltíonn an oifig seo roimh fhiosrúcháin i nGaeilge.
