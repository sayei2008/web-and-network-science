Volunteering & Student Engagement

There are opportunities for students to volunteer in a myriad of organisations and events, so it should be quite feasible for most students or graduates to gain relevant experience for their CV – contact the Careers Office for information on local volunteering opportunities.

Student Ambassador Programme

Are you interested in becoming an LYIT Student Ambassador? Do you feel that you have what it takes to represent LYIT at off-campus events and support LYIT on-campus activties? Would you like to return to your former school and talk about your student experience at LYIT? Then if so, why not join the LYIT Student Ambassador Team.

Why join?

  • Improve your communication & organisational skills
  • Gain experience in event management
  • Create fantastic networks and connections for the future
  • Enhance your CV.

The Commitment – what’s involved?

An LYIT student ambassador will support 1-2 activities per semester.

Application Process

To download an application form click here.

Please return completed application form by e-mail to Fiona at fiona.kelly@lyit.ie.

Contact Us

For further information contact Fiona at fiona.kelly@lyit.ie.

Be part of this exciting initiative and join the LYIT Student Ambassador Team today.
