Explore Killybegs

Our Killybegs Campus is set in some of the most unspoiled countryside and coastal areas of Donegal.

With a stunning coastline and sweeping Blue Flagged beaches on your doorstep no wonder it’s home to the LYIT School of Tourism.

Immerse yourself in its breathtaking beauty and be inspired to learn.

With a diverse range of transport links, our Killybegs campus is easily accessible for national and international students and visitors.
