Strategic Plan

LYIT has a clear, ambitious, and consistent strategic vision and stands apart from other HEIs in terms of its distinctive mission and its commitment to the border region it serves. LYIT strengths are strong regional engagement, flexibility in meeting national policy objectives, broadening higher education participation, quality of programmes delivered, and the excellent standing the Institute has with students, alumni, enterprise and other regional stakeholders.

Currently the Institute is undertaking a  consultation process in development of the LYIT's next Strategic Plan for the period 2018/22.
