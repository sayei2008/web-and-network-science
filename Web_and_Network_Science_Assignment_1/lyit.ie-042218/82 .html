New Frontiers Entrepreneur Programme

New Frontiers is Ireland’s national entrepreneur development programme.

Developed by Enterprise Ireland and delivered locally by LYIT, at its core is a groundbreaking six-month programme designed to turn great ideas into viable businesses, focusing on helping you to develop vital entrepreneurial skills to succeed.

It provides you with training, mentoring, networking opportunities and space in CoLab (the Institute’s incubation centre) to help you establish and run your own company.

Successful participants will also receive financial support of €15,000 from Enterprise Ireland as they focus all their efforts on developing a business during this six-month period.

The New Frontiers programme is now inviting applications. To apply contact Patsy Donaghy, CoLab Manager at  patrick.donaghey@lyit.ie / colab@lyit.ie or +353 (0)74 918 6703.
